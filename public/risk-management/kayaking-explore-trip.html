Risk Management

Although kayaking is a safe sport, like any other sports, things can go wrong in kayaking as well. Every time we take our kayaks out, we prepare for the worst.

Measures taken for the safety of the participants

  • First Aid Kit

    The guide for the kayaking trip carries a first aid box along with him all the time

  • Life Jacket

    All the participants along with the guide are required to wear life jacket/PFD while going in the water.

  • Proper hydration

    All the participants are provided 2 liters of water to ensure proper hydration throughout the journey.

  • Sun Burn

    All the participants are provided with a fishermen cap and we ask all the participants to wear full sleeve t-shirt and full sleeve pajama/pant.

Injury management

In case of any injury to the participants following steps are taken

  • In the case of minor injury, first aid is done and the injury is observed for some time. If participants do not get better we rescue the participants through the nearest rescue location.
  • In the case of major injury, first aid is done and an ambulance is called. Participant is taken to the nearest hospital as soon as possible.

Rescue Location

We have identified rescue location along the kayaking route to rescue the participants in case of serious injury.

Measures taken for the safety of the Equipment

Our safety depends on the reliable equipment. We check all our equipment every time before using it on the journey
