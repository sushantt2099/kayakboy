Famous Place nearby Mulki

  • Beach

    • Kapu Beach(Udupi) - 20 km - lots of shop/restaurant nearby beach - A very famous and beautiful beach with working light house
    • Sasihithlu Beach - 12 km - No shop/restaurant near beach - A very beautiful beach only known by the locals
    • New Tannirbhavi Beach(Mangalore) - 27km - This beautiful beach is in mangalore, need to take Ferry from sultan bathery in Mangalore

  • Hill Station

    • Kudremukha - 80 km - Heaven Hill Station very close to Mulki.
    • Agumbe - 85 km - Hill Station with very narrow road.

  • Old Monuments

    • St. Lawrence Church(Karkala) - 35 km - 250 years beautiful old church.
    • Shri Bhagwan Bahubali Digambar Jain Statue(Karkala) - 40 km - around 600 years old 42 ft statue on top of a rocky hill built.
