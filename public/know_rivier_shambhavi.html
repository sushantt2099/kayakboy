Know River Shambhavi

River Shambhavi is one of the many river that originates from western ghats(kudremukha) and meets the Arabian sea.

Shambhavi river is approximately 50 km long. River starts with very narrow(5 - 10 meters) width. As it gets closer to the Arabian sea the width increases. At some point river is upto 500 meters wide.

Its has very fast current during monsoon season(June - September), it is advised from the authority to stay away from the river during monsoon season.

Apart from the monsoon season the river is very calm and quite. River has lush green trees across its length.

River Shambhavi is one of the most hidden river, it is hardly known or seen my anyone.
