On this trip, I will take you to an Isolated and pristine beach at Mulki, India. Here river Shambhavi meets the Arabian Sea.

Beach is beautiful, with crystal clear water. Waves from the sea break on one side and the river silently flows on the other side of the beach.

Beach is full of the sea shell, and the orange sky during the sunset makes it a truly mesmerizing experience.

I take individual and group for kayaking to the beach. Total distance is 4 km(including both ways). We leave the kayak on the river side of the beach, where there is no current in the water. And then walk towards the sea. We take the kayak and head back after the sunset.
