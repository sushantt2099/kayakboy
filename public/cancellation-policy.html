Cancellation Policy

100% refund will be made if event is cancelled by the organiser due to unavoidable reasons.

90% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled 7 days before the event

70% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled 7 days before the event

60% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled 72 hoursbefore the event

50% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled 48 hoursbefore the event

40% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled 12 hoursbefore the event

No refund will be made in case of no show or cancellation after the event starts
