Cancellation Policy

100% refund will be made if event is cancelled by the organiser due to unavoidable reasons.

90% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled before 7 days hours from the event

70% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled after 7 days from the event

60% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled after 72 hours from the event

50% refund will be made if ticket is cancelled after 24 hours from the event

No refund will be made in case of no show or cancellation after the event starts
