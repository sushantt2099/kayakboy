My First Solo Kayaking Trip

During the time I had a work from home job and I was living in Mulki, Mangalore. Mulki is 30 km from Mangalore and around 350 km from Bangalore.

I had already bought a kayak for myself around a month back. It was a sit-on-top recreational kayak from Mission.

My house was nearby a river named Shambhavi, where I learned to paddle. Initially, I use to paddle 2-5 kilometers at a time.

After kayaking few weeks, I was confident enough to go for a longer solo trip. At first, I planned to go saint mary island in Udupi. Later on, I decided that It will be much cooler and fun to go and find the start of river Shambhavi.

I have watched this river from past 8 years. I have felt the cool refreshing breeze from the river during the bus ride from my college to Mangalore. I use to look out the window and try to see the river as far in the river as I could. Dense Green mangrove and coconut trees on both sides of the river was a scenic beauty.

This will be my attempt to start from one end of the river and try to go as far as I can. This was the beginning of my kayaking journey.
