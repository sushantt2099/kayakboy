The Plan: Kayaking in River Suvarna

Introduction

Every year start of the season I want to host a unique kayaking tour. This tour will have limited seat and will also be non-profit.

2017 will be the first year that I will be doing it. This year we are going to kayak in river Suvarna.

River Suvarna is a beauty that very few or nobody might have seen before. It starts from the lush dense forest of western ghats and cuts through the rocky terrain to meet Arabian sea. Western ghats are known to be older than the mighty Himalayas. So should be river Suvarna. River Suvarna could be flowing from 100 of millions of years.

There are 8 seats for this event. Contact me to get a seat for yourself.

Event Date: October 27, 28, 29, 2017

Total Distance: 65km

Total Time: 3 Day + 2 Night

River Dams:

  • Baje Hydraulic Power(Under Construction)( we will cross only this.)

Camping Location

  • Day 1: Puttige Mutt Udipi or nearby location(will have to paddle 25km)
  • Day 2: End Point of the route. (distance 15 km we can take rest and cook nice food here). I am expecting this place to be extraordinary with natural swimming pool of size 2x the olympic pool. Also the place is rocky so nice and neat for camping.

Map

Itinerary

Day 1

Start early around 6 AM

Paddle with the tide 25km starting from Kodi Bengare Beach

Camp near Puttige Mutt Udupi

Day 2

Start early around 6 AM

Paddle for 15 km to the end location

Camp near the end point it is not rocky location with natural swimming pool formation

Day 3

Start early around 6 AM

Return Back, paddle 25 km

Call the truck and head back home.

  • Food with 4000 calories/day
  • Stove and cooking utensils
  • First Aid kit
  • Hammock
